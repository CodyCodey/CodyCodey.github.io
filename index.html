What is happening?

People are going to starve this coming winter. There is mass famine on the cards for countries. African and Middle Eastern countries will bear the brunt of this famine.

Why Famine?

Simple Answer: War. In Ukraine. Ukraine was the top exporter of wheat grains before the war. Since the war has commenced, 20 million tonnes of wheat remain stuck inside country. Negotiations to move this wheat safely are going nowhere. Export bans by countries are not helping.

Why Help?

The pandemic killed 7-8 million people. Majority of them old. This global famine will kill even more. Majority of them children. Most of them are still going to die. But perhaps your contributions will save a few.
